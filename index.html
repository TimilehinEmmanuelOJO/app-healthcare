Cold Chain Monitoring for Healthcare
Most pharmaceutical products like vaccines, diluents etc. are temperature sensitive. They require special handling from the moment they are manufactured to when they are stored, transported and distributed to the final consumer. For them to remain potent, temperature sensitive pharmaceuticals must be kept within the cold chain at the manufacturers recommended temperature.

Therefore it is compulsory to both maintain the cold chain using a network of cold stores, freezers and other refrigerated systems, and to monitor the condition of pharmaceuticals during their storage, transport and distribution, in real time.
